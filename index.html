What do we prioritize?

  • Simple, secure devops
  • Intuitive developer experience
  • Making the complicated easy
  • Supporting open source

Every container you deploy gets a memory and CPU resource allocation. You can specify these in your .kubesimple file or we'll pick a sensible default for you. The total resource allocation for all containers you schedule cannot exceed your plan limit.

To make your app highly available, we will run multiple processes (pods and deployments) and load balance your traffic between them. If your app fails, we will start a new one. Databases in a highly available configuration feature automatic failover and periodic backups. In enterprise (scaling) plans, we deploy your app and database across multiple regions so that an outage in any one region will not affect your app.

When you deploy your app via a .kubesimple file, it is converted to a Helm chart using Kubernetes best practices for security and availability. You can always download this chart and use it to deploy your app on your own cluster with full control over the configuration.
